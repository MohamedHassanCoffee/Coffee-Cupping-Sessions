Welcome to Cupping Sessions

Explore the world of specialty coffee through our immersive cupping sessions. Learn about coffee's history, cultivation, and preparation methods while tasting some of the finest coffee varieties.

  • Coffee history, plant description, and cultivation specifics
  • Overview of coffee-producing countries and regions
  • Processing methods and their impact on taste
  • Factors influencing coffee characteristics
  • Specialty grade coffee and cupping protocols
  • Visit a roasting facility and taste coffee using the flavor wheel
  • Hands-on experience with coffee preparation methods
  • Fun activities with aroma kits and coffee trivia
